Membership Privileges

Does members Get Special Benefits?
Ofcourse Yes!!! By joining the TravelloPEDIA membership program, you'll receive special member-only benefits like a 10% discount every time you play golf at Top Golf, a 10% discount on ticket if you fly international, Free breakfast in the hotel you will stay in if you book from our site, free drinks in the entire journey of your cruise and many more. Please read the different types of memberships below

What are the different types of memberships?
There are three types of memberships - Diamond, Platinum and Gold.

Gold - You can become our Gold Member at $25 and $10 for extending the membership every year. Gold offers you very few privilages. You get a 5% discount on domastic flight's ticket and a 10% discount on an international flight's ticket.

Platinum - You can become our Platinum Member at $75 and $5 for extending the membership every year. You get a 5% discount on domastic flight's ticket, a 10% discount on an international flight's ticket, Free breakfast in the hotel you will stay in if you book from our site.

Diamond - You can become our Diamond Member at $500 for life time and no extention fee. You get a 5% discount on domastic flight's ticket, a 10% discount on an international flight's ticket, Free breakfast and lunch in the hotel you will stay in if you book from our site, a 10% discount every time you play golf at Top Golf and free drinks in the entire journey of your cruise.
